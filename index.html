About Me

Hi, I'm Bhavya! I'm a high school student at Emerald High School with a passion for computational biology and neuroscience. I'm always excited about exploring the intersection of science and technology to solve real-world problems. In my free time, I enjoy coding, reading about emerging scientific breakthroughs, and creating engaging projects that bring my ideas to life.

Mission Statement

Throughout my educational years, I have been passionate and dedicated toward improving the lives of disabled people. This journey began in eighth grade when my mom secured a teaching position where she taught math and science to students with disabilities, allowing me to witness firsthand the challenges that these individuals face. This inspired me to pursue community service and work on various research projects that are outlined in this portfolio. I am eager to continue innovative research in many ways, aiming to leverage AI and machine learning to improve the lives of individuals with disabilities.
